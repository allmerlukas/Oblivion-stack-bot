1

Who We Are

Oblivion ("the Bot", "we", "our") is a Discord bot providing partner management, auto-wave ad exchange, giveaway management, ticket systems, and general server moderation tools.

The Bot is operated by its developer ("Developer"). For contact information, see Section 10.

2

Data We Collect

Oblivion only collects data that is strictly necessary to provide its features. The table below lists every type of data stored:

Data Type Examples Purpose Stored?
Guild (Server) IDs Server identifier Per-server configuration Yes
Channel IDs Partner/ad/log channel Auto-Wave routing Yes
Role IDs Member/ping roles Ping tier logic Yes
User IDs Giveaway host, ticket owner Giveaways, tickets, partner tracking Yes
Usernames Discord username Partner link display Yes
Message content Ad text in ad channel Read-only for Auto-Wave sends; never stored permanently Read only
Partner URLs Server invite links Partner link tracking / deduplication Yes
⚠️
Oblivion does not read general message content in your server. It only reads messages in the specific channel you designate as your ad channel via /config setup.
3

Why We Collect It

All data collection is tied to a specific bot feature:

  • Guild & channel IDs — to route Auto-Wave partner ads to the correct channels.
  • Role IDs — to determine the correct ping tier when a partner ad is sent.
  • User IDs & usernames — to associate giveaways and tickets with the user who created them, and to display partner statistics.
  • Partner URLs — to deduplicate partner links and provide per-day partnership statistics.
4

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally in a SQLite database on the server running the bot. The database file is:

  • Not publicly accessible.
  • Not uploaded to any cloud database or external service.
  • Protected by the server's own access controls.
✅
No external database providers (e.g. Firebase, MongoDB Atlas, Supabase) are used. Your data stays on the bot's host machine.
5

Data Retention

  • Guild configuration — retained while the bot remains in your server. Removing the bot does not automatically delete your config; you may request deletion (see Section 7).
  • Giveaways — deleted automatically when the giveaway ends.
  • Tickets — deleted when the ticket channel is closed.
  • Partner links — daily records are pruned after 30 days automatically.
  • Error logs — a rolling window of the most recent 200 errors; older entries are deleted automatically.
6

Third Parties

Oblivion does not share your data with any third party. The only external service the bot communicates with is Discord's API (api.discord.com), which is required for the bot to function. Discord's own privacy policy applies to data held by Discord.

7

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a summary of what data is stored for your guild or user ID.
  • Deletion — request that all data associated with your guild or user ID be deleted.
  • Correction — update any stored data via the bot's slash commands (e.g. /config setup).

To exercise these rights, contact us via the information in Section 10.

8

Children's Privacy

Oblivion is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Discord's own Terms of Service require users to be at least 13 years old.

9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
